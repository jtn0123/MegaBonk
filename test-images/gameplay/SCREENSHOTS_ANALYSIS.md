## Known Challenges

1. **Cyrillic text** (Screenshot 2): OCR may struggle with Russian characters
2. **Visual effects**: Heavy particle effects may obscure item icons
3. **Small icons**: Items are relatively small in the inventory bar
4. **Duplicate counting**: Many items have x2, x3, x5 counts - need to detect count numbers
5. **Multi-row inventories**: Screenshot 2 has 3 rows - need to handle variable layouts
